ABOUT ME

T'Izzy Mua was born when passion turned into a way of living. I decided that I want to make every woman feel beautiful and the only way to do so was to make my self known. I started when I was 13, using my mothers eyeshodow pallet, I would go around the block and ask my friends to let me make them pretty. It was so much fun! Then, years passed by and I wanted to start my own business. At the beginning most of the projects were vice-versa promoting, I would collaborate with a photographer and get my own portfolio.Have fun looking through pictures and give me the opportunity to make you feel the most beautiful in the world by redifining your own beauty!
